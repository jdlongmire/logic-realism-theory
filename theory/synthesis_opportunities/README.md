# Synthesis Opportunities

This folder contains documents tracking ideas and content from past work that could be synthesized into current papers or development.

**Created**: Session 36.0 (2025-12-02)
**Source**: All-work survey of ~392 files

---

## Purpose

During the comprehensive survey of archived and past work, several valuable ideas were identified that:
- Were developed but not integrated into current papers
- Represent alternative approaches worth preserving
- Could strengthen current arguments if synthesized properly

## Categories of Opportunities

1. **Paper Content Gaps** - Ideas not in current papers
2. **Derivation Strengthening** - Arguments that could be improved
3. **Alternative Framings** - Different ways to present same concepts
4. **Transferable Proofs** - Formal work from earlier approaches
5. **Experimental Proposals** - Tests that weren't fully developed
