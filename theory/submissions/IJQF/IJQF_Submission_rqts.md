## Scope

The journal is dedicated to all aspects of the foundations of quantum theories, including:
- Conceptual foundations of quantum mechanics
- Mathematical foundations of quantum mechanics
- Quantum field theory foundations
- Quantum gravity foundations

**Aim:** To promote quantum foundations research by providing a problem-oriented and debate-encouraged forum for researchers.

## How to Submit

### Option 1: IJQF Members
- Log in to IJQF
- Post paper as PDF attachment in the Blogs section
- Write relevant introduction in post

### Option 2: Non-Members
- Send email with:
  - Cover letter
  - Paper attachment (PDF format)

## Article Processing Charges (APC)

| Author Type | APC |
|-------------|-----|
| Premier IJQF members | **Free** |
| Authors with grant funding | $600 USD |
| Authors without grant funding | $300 USD |

**Note:** APC is mandatory for accepted papers (unless Premier member).
