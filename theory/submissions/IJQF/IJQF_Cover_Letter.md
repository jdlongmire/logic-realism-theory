**Summary**

This paper introduces Logic Realism Theory (LRT), a framework proposing that physical reality is co-constituted by logical constraint operating on information space. The central thesis decomposes into:

- **Theorem 1** (empirically supported): No completed measurement violates the Three Fundamental Laws of Logic (Identity, Non-Contradiction, Excluded Middle)
- **Conjecture 1** (open): Logical constraint on information suffices for physical actualization

**Relevance to IJQF**

The manuscript engages directly with topics central to quantum foundations:
