## Session 23.0 Work

### Empirical Challenge Reframing

**Problem identified:** Paper's philosophical apparatus provides escape routes from the central empirical confrontation. Reviewers can engage philosophically without ever confronting the brute fact that no physical measurement has ever violated 3FLL.

**Solution implemented:** Lead and conclude with the empirical challenge.

#### Section 1.1 "The Challenge" (new, +35 lines)

Opens with:
- Universal pattern: every measurement yields one determinate outcome
- Asymmetry table: imagination generates violations easily, reality never does
- Explicit challenge: produce one counterexample
- Stakes: framework explains the fact, but the fact is the anchor

Key passage:
> **The Challenge:** Identify one physical measurement, at any scale, in any domain, at any point in history, that violated the Law of Identity, Non-Contradiction, or Excluded Middle.
>
> No such measurement exists.

#### Section 32.6 "The Challenge Renewed" (+20 lines)

Concludes with:
- Challenge restated
- Acknowledgment of alternative explanations (none superior)
- Final framing: question is not *whether* reality respects logic, but *why*
