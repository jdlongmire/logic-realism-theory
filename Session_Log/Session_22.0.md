## Comprehensive Paper Breakdown

### CORE THESIS

**A = L(I)** - Actuality equals logical filtering applied to Infinite Information Space

The Three Fundamental Logical Laws (3FLL) - Identity, Non-Contradiction, Excluded Middle - are not cognitive conventions but **ontological constraints constituting physical distinguishability**. Quantum mechanics emerges as the unique stable interface between non-Boolean IIS and Boolean actuality.

---

### PART I: PHILOSOPHICAL FOUNDATIONS (Sections 1-7)

**Section 1: Introduction**
- Central formula: A = L(I)
- Distinction: selection claim, not derivation claim
- LRT does NOT claim to derive QM from logic alone

**Section 2: The Three Fundamental Logical Laws**
- Identity: ∀x: x = x
- Non-Contradiction: ∀x,P: ¬(P(x) ∧ ¬P(x))
- Excluded Middle: ∀x,P: P(x) ∨ ¬P(x)
- Historical context: Aristotle through modern challenges (intuitionism, paraconsistency, dialetheism)

**Section 3: Central Argument (Inference to Best Explanation)**
- Observation 1: Universal compliance - no physical violation of 3FLL ever observed
- Observation 2: Detection capacity - we could detect violations if they occurred
- Six candidate explanations; only "constitutive constraint" survives

**Section 4: Infinite Information Space (IIS)**
- IIS = totality of distinguishable states (co-primitive with 3FLL)
- Not Platonic, not set-theoretic, not epistemic
- "Information" = distinguishability structure (Bateson: "difference that makes a difference")

**Section 5: Boolean Actuality**
- Actuality = domain where 3FLL fully enforced
- IIS contains non-Boolean structure (superpositions, indeterminate states)
- Actuality must be Boolean - determinate, non-contradictory
- Interface problem: what mediates between IIS and actuality?

**Section 6: Consistency Bridging Principle (CBP)**
- CBP: Admissible transformations on IIS must permit consistent Boolean resolution
- Not a collapse postulate - constrains dynamics, not measurement
- Implications: reversibility, continuity

**Section 7: Objections and Responses**
- Unfalsifiability → framework claim with transcendental falsifiers
- Category error → logical structure instantiated in reality
- Kantian alternative → cannot explain mathematical effectiveness
- Epistemic gap → transcendental-ontological claim
- Quantum mechanics → superposition ≠ contradiction
- Dialetheism → no dialetheic physics exists
